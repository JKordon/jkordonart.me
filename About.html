About Me

I’m Jackson. Baltimore/Cleveland based artist, photographer/videographer, editor, digital restorationist, and general rascal. I’ve lived at 47.1915° N, 52.8371° W for the past 3 years of my existence. My work typically consists of criticisms directed at the Military Industrial Complex, the heavily debated existence of Ghouls, Goblins, and Gnomes, and of course, the obligatory Breaking Bad reference.

Much like the mighty Capybara, I bask the camera in the glory of the sun, using my two scent glands to find the perfect settings for its ISO and lighting. The rest comes straight from my meerkat’s soul and antelope esophagus. I out-pizza’d the Hut and now the CIA is trying to assassinate me.
